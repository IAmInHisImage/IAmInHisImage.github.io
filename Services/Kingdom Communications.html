Kingdom Communicationss




Kingdom Communications teaches Spanish courses to churches and individuals in the Upstate. The course is 20-weeks and is taught to small groups of twelve. It is our hope to bridge the communication gap between the Hispanic and English culture.

Kingdom Communicationss also provides training courses to churches, daycares, and professionals that will enhance team building, communication, social, emotional skills, etc. These courses provide participants with DSS accredited hours.
