Encouraging Words

Encouraging Words was birthed in 2001. Initially the visionary made name definition pieces to give as gifts to family and friends. Before long the name definition and inspirational pieces were being purchased and given to individuals and couples all over the Upstate. As a result, Encouraging Word added bookmarks and inspirational calendars to the product list. Since 2001 all proceeds from Encouraging Words are used to fund other In His Image services.

It is oure desire to expand Encourageing Words to a Bookstore that will sell the following items:

  • Books written by inspirational writers, participants enrolled in other In His Image services, and local authors
  • Publications
  • Various Magazines
  • Keepsakes
  • Promotional Items
  • Artwork
  • School Supplies
  • CD's
  • DVD's
