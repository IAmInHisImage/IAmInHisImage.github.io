Our Vision

The Vision of In His Image is to present to God a people who will pursue Christ-likeness, become like Christ, and be guided and counseled by Holy Spirit to fulfill the purpose God predestined for them in life, and assist others in the world to be like Christ. In His Image wants to be recognized as a source in the upstate and surrounding areas, Ga., NC, and nationally for finding, developing, and positioning well prepared champions for Christ that impact the world

Our Mission Statement

The mission of In His Image is to assist participants in understanding and reaching God’s plan for our lives by teaching them to be like Christ - as God has created man “In His Image” – by offering Christ-centered physical, mental, social, and spiritual educational teachings, and Biblical sessions.
