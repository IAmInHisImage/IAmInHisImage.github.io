Our goal here at In His Image

  • Increase the individual’s, families’, and communities’ knowledge of who God has called them to be
  • Increase the individual’s, families’ and communities’ knowledge of the Christ-like character so they can be transformed into the image of Christ.
  • Increase the individual’s, families’, and communities’, knowledge of the Holy Spirit so they will hear Him and do as He says.
  • Promote the teaching of biblical principals by demonstrating the Bible’s principles relate to all aspects of life.
  • Promote the teaching of academics by demonstrating how core and extracurricular academic courses will help them to become who God created us to be.
