Who is in His Image?


In His Image is a place to come and learn who you are and how you fit into God's plan for our world. There is something for everyone at In His Image. Whether you are young or old; want to learn more about being Christ-like; or just looking for a new place to learn more about life, In His Image is here to help you!


Come be a part of one of our six educational services. The atmosphere at In His Image is relaxed. Reguardless of what service or event, In His Image strives to provide relevant information so that people can learn to live life abundantly. Through meaningful relationships, biblical studies, worship, and interactive activities, In His Image aspires to help individuals, families, and communities grow to be the wonderful persons who God created us to be.


There is a service for people from all walks of life. Our services are tailored for all members of our family. We have a Christian school, emotional health servies, special events, and many more events throughout the year, so that there are many opportunites to get involved. Take a look around, and don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions. In His Image would love to meet you soon!

You don't have to live an ordinary life. God has a plan that will give you abundant life. Come let's figure that life out together!
