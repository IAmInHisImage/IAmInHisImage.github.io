SERVICES PROVIDED BY IN HIS IMAGE


SIX EDUCATIONAL SERVICES


Creative Sunsets

Provides high quality classes to children ages 5 -7 and their families that are aimed at reducing negative health, social, and communication consequences resulting from maltreatment, sexual, physical abuse, mental abuse, neglect, poverty, and other high risk factors.

LEARN MORE

Encouraging Words

Provides encouragement by publicizing and purchasing books, affirmations, calendars, name definition pieces, etc. that affirm who participants are in Christ.

LEARN MORE

Lay Aside Every Weight

Provides individuals, families, and communities with coaching sessions and bible studies that help them find out who God created them to be.

LEARN MORE

Kingdom Communications

Provides individuals, families, and communities with workshops and conferences to groups to enhance personal growth and development or to meet training requirements to maintain certification and/or licensure.

LEARN MORE

King's Kids

Is the behavioral redirection service of In His Image. It provides individual sessions for ages 12-18 that will help them learn to deal with anger, low self-esteem, authority issues, etc. It also provides total abstinence courses during after school hours and the summer which is aimed at reducing premarital sex.

LEARN MORE

Trinity Christian Educational School

Service provides academic, emotional, social and spiritual education to all ages K5 - 12th grade. TCES operates in compliance with South Carolina Department of Education academic requirements and is a member of SCACS.
